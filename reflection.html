Reflection!

I am a marketing major. I currently work as a graphic design lead for two internships in the Columbia area. One is affiliated with Mizzou and the other is with Texas Roadhouse. I truly love creating and organizing, my passion makes me the perfect fit. I’m an extremely organized person, meaning I always have due dates on track. I also have pride for everything that my name is attached to. Both of these qualities assure quality and consistency in my work.

My personal design style is a mixture of bohemian and art deco style. I use many bright colors and retro fonts that give a strong bohemian vibe. Strong line work and geometrical shapes can also be seen in my work that show the art deco style. My personality shines in my work which creates the unique product I create. My humor and charm are shown in the bright colors and eccentric fonts I often use in personal designs. My photography also captures these styles and moods. My work often has a 70’s Woodstock vibe that is very trendy in modern design. This is a style I utilize frequently.
